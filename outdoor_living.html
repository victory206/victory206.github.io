Outdoor Living Spaces

How can we help?

At Elliott Bay Construction, we're here to transform your outdoor dreams into reality. Whether it's a cozy deck, a stylish patio, or an expansive outdoor living space, we've got you covered. Here's how we make it happen:

Design Consultation:
  • Tailored Solutions:We craft a customized plan that aligns with your budget, timeline, and design aspirations.
  • Material Selection: From durable hardwoods to low-maintenance composite decking, we guide you in selecting the perfect materials that suit your style and climate.
  • Permit Assistance: We handle all necessary permits and regulatory requirements, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free construction process.
Customized Planning:
  • Personalized Approach: We start by sitting down with you to understand your vision, preferences, and lifestyle needs.
  • Site Assessment: Our experts visit your property to assess the terrain, existing structures, and potential design opportunities.
  • Creative Solutions: Using our expertise, we propose innovative design ideas that maximize space and functionality while complementing your home's aesthetics.
Ongoing Support:
  • Personalized Approach: Your satisfaction is our priority. We provide ongoing support and maintenance advice to ensure your outdoor living space remains beautiful and functional for years to come.
  • Warranty Protection: We stand behind our workmanship with comprehensive warranty coverage, giving you peace of mind and confidence in your investment.
