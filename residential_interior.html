How can we help?

At Elliott Bay Construction, we help you transform your space into your dream home. From modern updates to classical redesigns, we guide your imagination into reality. Here are some ways we can be of service:

Design and Planning:
  • Needs Assessment: We begin by understanding your lifestyle, preferences, and specific needs. This helps us identify how each space should function and what improvements are necessary.
  • Priority-Based Budgeting: Selecting systems, appliances and materials that meet your budget while exceeding your expectations is something we go the extra mile for. Our goal is to help you create the space of your dreams without breaking the bank.
  • Space Optimization: We come up with creative solutions to help you maximize your space and storage and create multifunctional interior spaces. We value flexibility and incorporate ergonomic principles to make daily activities more convenient.
Construction and Finishing:
  • Attention to Detail: “Do it right or do it twice”, our team understands that things are built to last, not to change. We go above and beyond to ensure our final products reflect our commitment to excellence.
  • Smart Home Integration: Keeping an eye on new technology and appliances, we can help your space feel cutting edge by helping you choose items that excite you every use.
