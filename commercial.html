Restrooms

We specialize in building commercial bathrooms that cater to diverse needs and preferences, from simple and functional designs to elegant spaces with premium finishes. Our functional bathrooms focus on efficiency and practicality, maximizing space and ease of use with durable fixtures and materials ideal for high-traffic areas. For those seeking luxury, our elegant bathrooms feature high-quality materials, modern fixtures, and stylish accents, creating a refined atmosphere with meticulous attention to detail. Regardless of style, all our bathrooms are built with a commitment to quality and durability, ensuring they not only look impressive but also stand the test of time. We understand that a well-designed bathroom can significantly impact the user experience, and we are dedicated to creating spaces that meet the highest standards of functionality and elegance.

How can we help?

At Elliott Bay Construction, we're here to help take your commercial space to the next level. Whether it's a new conference room, office space, or a bathroom retrofit, we can help your business find a solution that reflects your values, and maximizes your space. Here are some ways we make that happen:

Design Consultation:
  • Construction Methods: We can work in conjunction with your architect to find a design that meets all your needs while also keeping constructability in mind.
  • Value Engineering: We will help you select materials and plan your layout to maximize how far your dollar goes in building out your commercial space.
Customized Planning:
  • Personalized Approach: We start by sitting down with you to understand your business’ needs and figure out a way to work symbiotically to negate the necessity to halt operations. We will work closely together to formulate any project phasing and scheduling that will be necessary for success.
  • Site Assessment: We visit your property to assess the area, planning laydown areas, site layout and ensuring proper safety measures are thought out before any construction takes place.
  • Creative Solutions: Using our expertise, we propose innovative design ideas and collaborate with all players involved to ensure obstacles are overcome without any negative consequences.
